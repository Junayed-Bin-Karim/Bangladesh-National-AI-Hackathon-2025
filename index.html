Bangladesh National AI Hackathon – 2025

Empowering university students to innovate and build real-world AI solutions addressing critical national challenges

Event Overview

What is AI for Bangladesh Hackathon?

This event challenges teams of undergraduate students to develop AI-based solutions for real-life problems in Bangladesh — from 🌿 environmental monitoring, 🌧️ disaster prediction, 📚 education technology, to 🏥 healthcare innovation.

Participants will submit their ideas online and compete in an offline final round.

Who Can Participate?

  • ✅ Teams of 2 undergraduate students
  • ✅ Team members may be from different departments or universities
  • ✅ Students from Engineering, Computer Science, Business, Science and related fields are encouraged
  • ✅ One student can be part of only one team
  • ✅ Each team must assign a Team Leader for communication
