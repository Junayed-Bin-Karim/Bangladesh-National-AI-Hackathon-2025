Event Overview

What is Bangladesh National AI Hackathon-2025?

This event challenges teams of undergraduate students to develop AI-based solutions for real-life problems in Bangladesh ‚Äî from environmental monitoring, disaster prediction, education technology, to healthcare innovation.

In the first round, participants will submit their ideas online. In the second round, 100 teams will be preliminarily selected. These selected teams must register again with a fee of 100 BDT to compete in the final round. From these 100 finalists, the top 3 teams will be selected as winners.

Who Can Participate?

  • Teams of 2 undergraduate students
  • Team members may be from different departments or universities
  • Students from Engineering, Computer Science, Business, Science and related fields are encouraged
  • One student can be part of only one team
  • Each team must assign a Team Leader for communication
