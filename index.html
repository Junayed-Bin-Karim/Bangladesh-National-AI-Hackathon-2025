Event Overview

About Bangladesh National AI Hackathon ‚Äì 2025

The Bangladesh National AI Hackathon ‚Äì 2025 is a prestigious national-level online competition inviting undergraduate students to develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges in Bangladesh through the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

This event offers a unique opportunity for aspiring innovators passionate about AI and problem-solving to showcase their creativity and make a tangible impact.

Focus Areas for AI Solutions

  • Environment: Addressing issues such as pollution control and waste management.
  • Disaster Alerts: Developing systems for flood, fire, and other emergency warnings.
  • Education: Creating AI-powered tools for learning support and tutoring.
  • Healthcare: Innovating in disease detection, mental health support, and related areas.

üåÄ How It Works

Round 1 ‚Äì Idea Submission

All participating teams must submit their AI-based idea online via a Google Form.

Your idea must be submitted as a PDF presentation, clearly outlining:

  • The problem you want to solve
  • Your AI-based solution
  • How your solution will create real impact in Bangladesh
  • Optional: Basic implementation plan, tools/technologies you might use

You do not need to code or build a prototype in this round.

Teams must work on a single fixed problem statement, which will be released on August 15 in the problem-statements Problem Statement section of this website.

Judging Criteria for Round 1:

  • Relevance to the chosen problem statement
  • Creativity and originality of the idea
  • Potential impact and feasibility
  • Clarity and quality of the presentation

Round 2 ‚Äì Final Presentation

The top 100 teams will be selected from Round 1 based on their idea submissions. These selected teams will receive an official confirmation email.

To proceed, each selected team must re-register by paying a 100 BDT confirmation fee (payment instructions will be shared via email).

In this round, teams will join an online presentation session to explain their idea in more detail to a panel of judges.

No working AI model or coding is required, but teams should:

  • Refine their original idea
  • Be ready to answer questions about its feasibility, social impact, and implementation plan

üéØ What to Prepare:

  • A short, improved PowerPoint or PDF presentation
  • Clear answers to potential questions from judges
  • Strong justification for why your solution matters

üèÜ Final Selection & Awards

After the Round 2 presentations, the judges will evaluate all 100 teams.

The top 3 teams will be selected as winners and awarded based on:

  • Innovation: How unique and creative the idea is
  • Impact: How effectively the solution addresses a real problem in Bangladesh
  • Clarity: How well the team explains the idea and its potential

üéñÔ∏è Special certificates will be given to all 100 finalists, and honorable mentions may be awarded to other promising teams.

Eligibility

  • The competition is open to all undergraduate students currently enrolled in universities across Bangladesh.
  • Teams should consist of 1 to 2 members.

Additional Information

  • The entire hackathon will be conducted fully online, allowing participation from any location.
  • Prior expertise in AI is not required; we encourage creative and innovative ideas from all participants.
  • Please ensure timely submission of your ideas before the deadline to be considered.
