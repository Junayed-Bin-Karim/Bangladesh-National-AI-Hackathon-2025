Event Overview

❓ What is Bangladesh National AI Hackathon – 2025?

This event challenges teams of undergraduate students to develop AI-based solutions for real-life problems in Bangladesh — from environmental monitoring, like checking air and water pollution and protecting forests; disaster prediction, such as warning systems for floods, cyclones, or earthquakes; education technology, like smart learning apps or tools to help students do better in school; to healthcare innovation, such as using AI to help doctors find diseases early, support patients in remote areas, and improve mental health care.

📥 In the first round, participants will submit their ideas online.

✅ In the second round, 100 teams will be preliminarily selected. These teams must re-register with a fee of 100 BDT to enter the final round.

🏆 From these finalists, the top 3 teams will be selected as winners.

Who Can Participate?

  • Teams of 2 undergraduate students
  • Team members may be from different departments or universities
  • Students from Engineering, Computer Science, Business, Science and related fields are encouraged
  • One student can be part of only one team
  • Each team must assign a Team Leader for communication
